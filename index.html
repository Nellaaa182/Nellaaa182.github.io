How to Make Alfredo Pasta

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • 3 slice of Smoked beef, cut
  • 3 Portobello Mushroom
  • 500 ml water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Â½ Minced Onion
  • 3 garlic cloves, chop
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • 150 ml Milk
  • Alfredo sauce

    • Tools/Materials

  • Stove
  • Non-Stick Pan
  • Spatula/Large Wooden Spoon

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove and then wait until the water boils.
  2. After the water boils, put the pasta into the pan.
  3. Cover the pan with the lid of the pan, wait for 45 minutes.
  4. After that, open the lid of the pan and turn off the stove.
  5. Drain the pasta and put them on the plate.
  6. Remove the boiled water into the sink.
  7. Turn the stove, and put the butter into the pan.
  8. Spread the butter until it melts.
  9. After the butter melts, put the garlic into the pan.
  10. Stir fry garlic until fragrant.
  11. Add smoked beef and then stir fry them with garlic and butter.
  12. Put Onion and Mushroom and Stir-fry them together.
  13. Gradually pour milk into the pan.
  14. Then put the pasta into the pan.
  15. Stir the pasta until it is mixed well.
  16. Add the Alfredo sauce and then stir with the large wooden spoon.
  17. Add salt and white pepper.
  18. Turn off your stove.
  19. Move your pasta into a plate.
  20. You can add the black pepper into your pasta.
